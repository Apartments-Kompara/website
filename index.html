About the Apartment

Welcome to our charming apartment located in the picturesque village of Lokavec, near Ajdovščina in the stunning Vipava Valley. This cozy retreat offers the perfect escape for those seeking tranquility and natural beauty.

Why Choose Lokavec?
Nestled in the heart of Slovenia's wine country, Lokavec offers breathtaking views of the valley, easy access to hiking trails, and proximity to charming local wineries. The area is known for its mild climate, excellent wines, and warm hospitality.

Apartment Features:

  • Fully equipped kitchen with modern appliances
  • Comfortable living area
  • Cozy bedroom with quality bedding
  • Modern bathroom
  • Free Wi-Fi and parking

The apartment is ideal for couples or small families looking to explore the Vipava Valley, enjoy outdoor activities, or simply relax in a peaceful setting away from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Points of Interest

Ajdovščina Town

2 km away

Historic town with Roman walls, local markets, and traditional restaurants. Explore the ancient Castra fortress ruins and enjoy authentic Slovenian cuisine.

Vipava Valley Wineries

Various locations nearby

Discover world-class wines in the numerous wineries scattered throughout the valley. The region is famous for its indigenous grape varieties and wine tourism.

Hiking & Cycling Trails

Starting from Lokavec

Access beautiful trails through vineyards, forests, and hills. The Upper Vipava Valley offers routes for all skill levels with stunning panoramic views.

Otlica Window

15 km away

A natural rock window offering spectacular views over the Vipava Valley. Popular destination for hikers and photographers.

Goriška Brda

30 km away

Slovenia's Tuscany - rolling hills covered with vineyards and orchards. Perfect for day trips, wine tasting, and scenic drives.

Ljubljana

70 km away

Slovenia's charming capital city with its picturesque old town, castle, and vibrant cultural scene. Easy day trip destination.

How to Get Here

✈️ Trieste Airport (TRS)

45 km away

Closest international airport in Italy. Convenient access with approximately 40 minutes drive, offering connections to major European cities.

✈️ Ljubljana Airport (LJU)

85 km away

Slovenia's main international airport. Approximately 1 hour drive, serving numerous European destinations with convenient connections.

✈️ Venice Airport (VCE)

150 km away

Marco Polo International Airport. About 1.5 hours drive, offering extensive international flight connections from a major Italian hub.
